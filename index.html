Who We Are

Capital at the
Moment of
Inception

Rahmanity Innovations, LLC is an early-stage investment vehicle focused on empowering exceptional founders from the earliest inflection points of their journey — pre-seed, seed, and Series A.

Founded by Nadeem Rahman, MD, Rahmanity brings a unique perspective bridging medicine, innovation, and enterprise. We believe the best companies are built at the intersection of deep domain knowledge and bold ambition.

We are more than capital — we are committed partners through every milestone.

Investment Focus

Where We Invest

◈

Pre-Seed

We back founders before the product is built — when conviction is currency. Our earliest bets go to those with clarity of problem and tenacity of purpose.

◈

Seed

At the seed stage, we help founders sharpen their go-to-market, build high-performing teams, and establish foundations needed to scale with precision.

◈

Series A

With product-market fit emerging, we provide strategic capital and network access needed to accelerate growth and define category leadership.

◇

Health & MedTech

With deep clinical expertise at our core, we are uniquely positioned to evaluate and support innovation in healthcare technology, diagnostics, and life sciences.

◇

Enterprise Software

We invest in B2B software solving real operational problems — from workflow automation to data infrastructure — with a sharp eye toward enterprise value creation.

◇

Emerging Technology

AI, frontier science, and platform businesses that reshape industries. We look for founders who see around corners and have the discipline to execute the vision.

President & CEO

MD

Nadeem Rahman, MD brings a rare combination of clinical expertise and entrepreneurial instinct to the world of early-stage investing. As founder and CEO of Rahmanity Innovations, he has built a platform dedicated to backing visionary founders at the earliest stages of company formation.

With deep roots in the Ohio innovation ecosystem through his affiliation with the Ohio Innovation Exchange and enterprise outreach leadership, Nadeem understands firsthand what it takes to translate breakthrough ideas into enduring companies.
